FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Baby Match.

Is Baby Match completely free?

Yes! Baby Match is 100% free with no in-app purchases or premium features. All functionality is available to everyone.

How many names are in the database?

Baby Match contains over 170,000 names from more than 100 countries and cultures around the world.

Can I use Baby Match with my partner?

Baby Match is designed for couples to use together. You can connect with your partner to see which names you both like.

Is Baby Match available on iOS?

Yes! Baby Match is available on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play Store) devices.
